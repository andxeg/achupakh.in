My goal is to demonstrate that deep learning systems are capable of much more than they can do today.
I've worked on training neural networks to generate speech and images, to play StarCraft and to solve reasoning tasks like coding and mathematics.
Common themes are generative models, deep reinforcement learning, imitation learning, and (most importantly) large-scale training.

I worked at DeepMind from 2017 to 2020, and now work at OpenAI in San Francisco.
Before getting into deep learning, I studied physics at the Large Hadron Collider at CERN.
